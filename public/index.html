About Us

Tarracore is Ireland's newest Farm and Plant machinery competition website. Located in the Midlands, our mission is to source the best Irish registered farm and plant machinery as prizes.

Our name is dervied from the Gaelic word for tractor, known as "Tarracóir". We decided to simplify the translation to make our brand easier to share across the web.

We are passionate about all things farm and agriculture related, especially tractors! Please help us get better, share your ideas on what you'd like to see as prizes.
